Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Grootman Haven Coffee was founded in 2015 by childhood friends Alex Morgan and Jamie Chen. What began as a shared passion for quality coffee in their college dorm room evolved into a dream of creating a community space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee in a welcoming environment.

Starting with just a small cart at the local farmer's market, we slowly built our reputation one perfectly crafted cup at a time. Through word of mouth and an uncompromising commitment to quality, we grew into the establishment we are today.

Our Coffee Journey

Every cup of Brew Haven coffee represents a journey that begins at carefully selected farms across the world's coffee-growing regions. We've built direct relationships with farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices.

Our master roaster, Maria Gonzalez, brings 15 years of experience to each batch, carefully developing profiles that highlight the natural flavors of each origin. Whether you prefer bright and fruity or deep and chocolatey, we have a roast that will delight your palate.
