Our Story

Founded in 2015, Grootman Haven Coffee began as a small passion project between two coffee enthusiasts. What started as a humble cart at the local farmer's market has grown into a beloved community hub for coffee lovers.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring fair trade practices and exceptional quality in every cup. Our master roasters carefully craft each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of the beans.
